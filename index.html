You might ask, "Why did you do that Chloe?"

I have no clue. That's a lie. I'm a liar. I do have a clue: I needed something to look forward to. It just so happened that that thing I'm looking forward to cost me 1000$ and my financial stability.

You might now be thinking, "Are you alright mentally?"

The short answer to this question would be yes, the long version of the same answer could possibly be interpreted as no.

Below you can see my Pros and Cons list and decide for yourself if you think I made the right decision:
    Cons
  1. My Saving account is now non-existent
  2. I have no money
  3. I will be an emotional wreck after the concert
    Pros
  1. I have something to look forward to
  2. I will get to see/hear my favorite songs being performed live
  3. Atleast I didn't spend the money on online shopping?
